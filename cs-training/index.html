Blend of web and smartphone.

One extra feature - The WowBox

WowBox Content

GrameenPhone and partners can provide different types of content through WowBox

  • Offers from GrameenPhone can be bought with takas or tokens.
  • Gifts are giveaways like free movie tickets or a coupon for a free tshirt.
  • Ads can also be delivered through the WowBox.

Tokens are earned when buying offers and visiting ads. Tokens can be used to buy new offers.

Offers

Offers

  • Can be bought with takas, tokens or for free
  • Works using Phone, SMS, USSD or URL
  • Can be throttled. For example "One offer per day for 30 first days"
  • Tokens are immediately rewarded to the user

Gifts

Gifts

  • They are free
  • They do not give any tokens
  • They can use a phone number, SMS or a url
  • Gifts make the WowBox more fun to use

Ads

Ads

  • They come from GrameenPhone or third party advertisers
  • The tokens does not come immediately, instead they are calculated once per day
  • They can use Phone, SMS or URL

Customer Service

  • Available on fxosad.telenordigital.com
  • Search for an individual WowBox user with phone number
  • See WowBox history for that user
  • If tokens have been spent wrongly, they can be redeemed by customer service
